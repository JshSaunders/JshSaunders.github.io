    About Us

    HR Equals People is an HR Outsource Solution Center that is driven by Core Strategic Competencies in the practice of Human Resources Management. We see Human Resources as having an integrated practice that must add value to the organizational effectiveness. Getting the practice of HR right from the inception is contingent on organizational effectiveness from a productivity, worker morale and organizational cohesion point of view. As such we do not see ourselves as consultants, we see ourselves as HR Leaders effecting positive change.

    We see all members of the organization as an important part of the success chains and competitive advantage of organizations. We help create necessary alignment in the practice of Human Resources Management through our various service offering.

    Our Mission:

    To deliver to our clientele relevant sustainable HR and Management Services through a facts based approach that is relevant and appropriate to their needs for Organizational and Systematic improvements.

    Our Strategic Intent:

    HR Equals People seeks to distinguish ourselves from other providers of HR and other related management services through our strategic intent and core values. We intend to be timely and responsive to all deliverables, with systems of continuous feedback. At no point shall our client will be uncertain of the status of projects as we aim at being proactive in anticipating the clientele needs and communicating such within reasonable time horizons and forums.

    Our Vision

    HR Equals People intends to be seen as the regional authority and provider for HR and Management services by expanding internal capacity and systems to address the regional needs.
